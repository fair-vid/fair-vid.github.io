The FAIR-VID Project

Artificial intelligence is increasingly applied in student admissions, yet many deployed systems remain difficult to inspect due to opaque cloud services. FAIR-VID presents a fully transparent AI framework that integrates multimodal data analysis, process mining, and an agent-based digital twin architecture.

Document Understanding

Zero-shot vision-language models + OCR reconciliation for international credentials from 100+ countries.

Video Interviews

Multimodal analysis of speech, behavior, prosody and content using fully local agents.

Process Digital Twin

Mined from real event logs. Transparent decision trees replace black-box predictions.
